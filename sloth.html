Sloth

Quite frankly, I don't have a favorite animal, per se. However, after watching Disney's Zootopia, I've come to find a new friend... The sloth! The perfomance delivered by the sloths on Zootopia was out of this world hilarious. Check out these fun facts on sloths:

  • Scientific Name: Folivora
  • Average Speed: .24 km/h
  • Average Lifespan: 20 - 30 years
  • Habitat: Jungles of Central and South America

At an average speed of .24 km/h, sloths are considered to be the slowest animals in the world.
